Jeremy Riddle

Who is Jeremy Riddle?

Jeremy Riddle is a Christian worship leader/songwriter. He leads at a church called Dwelling Place Anaheim, which was formerly called Vineyard Anaheim. It is located in Anaheim, California. Jeremy Riddle also made music with Bethel Music from 2011 to 2019.

What songs & albums has he released?

Jeremy Riddle has released many songs and albums, some under his own name and others under certain labels.
